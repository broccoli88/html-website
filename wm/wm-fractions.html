WM Fractions

  • Cygnar

    The Protectorate of Menoth's neighbor, Cygnar, is a nation of advanced technology and militaristic prowess. Their forces are characterized by lightning-powered warjacks, skilled gun mages, and versatile infantry units. They excel in ranged combat and possess strong battlefield control elements.

  • Khador

    Hailing from the frozen north, Khador is a powerful empire with heavily armored warjacks, elite infantry, and potent spells. They favor brutal melee attacks, and their warjacks can withstand immense punishment while delivering devastating blows.

    Khador models →

  • Protectorate of Menoth

    Protectorate of Menoth: A theocratic nation devoted to the god Menoth, their forces consist of heavily armored infantry, warjacks infused with holy power, and potent divine spells. They specialize in powerful synergies and burning away their foes with fire-based attacks.

    Protectorate of Menoth models →

  • Cryx

    An undead nation led by the dragon-litch lord Toruk, Cryx fields hordes of undead soldiers, vicious warjacks, and cunning warcasters. Their strength lies in their dark magic, stealthy maneuvers, and the ability to corrupt and weaken enemy forces.

    Cryx models →

  • Retribution of Scyrah

    A secretive and enigmatic faction, the Retribution of Scyrah fights to protect their homeland and magical artifacts. Their forces rely on fast-moving warjacks, skilled mage hunters, and specialized infantry. They are masters of precision and surgical strikes.

    Retribution of Scyrah models →

  • Convergence of Cyriss

    A faction of machine-worshiping cultists, the Convergence fields clockwork automatons and warcasters known as 'directors.' They have unique mechanics like the Induction system, which allows them to harness energy for powerful attacks.

    Convergence of Cyriss models →

  • Mercaneries

    A faction with a diverse assortment of warriors and warjacks hired from across the Iron Kingdoms. Mercenary armies can include units from multiple factions, offering players a wide range of tactical options.

    Mercaneries models →

  • Crucible Guard

    A military force of scientists, alchemists, and inventors from the Llaelese Resistance. They utilize alchemical concoctions, experimental warjacks, and specialized troops to achieve their goals.
