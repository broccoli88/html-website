Model Types

In Warmachine, there are several types of models that make up the armies of each faction. Each model type serves a specific role on the battlefield, and players must strategically use them to achieve their objectives. As of my last update in September 2021, here are the main model types in Warmachine:

  1. Warcaster: The central figure in each army, the warcaster is a powerful leader gifted with arcane abilities. They control the warjacks and cast spells to support their forces.

  2. Warjacks: Massive steam-powered mechanical constructs, warjacks are the centerpiece of each army. There are different classes of warjacks, such as light, heavy, and colossal, each with unique strengths and roles.

  3. Units: Units consist of groups of infantry or warriors that fight together as a cohesive force. They can be specialized in different roles, such as melee combat, ranged attacks, or support abilities.

  4. Solos: solos are individual models with unique abilities and often serve as specialists or champions. They can be warcasters' assistants, skilled warriors, or versatile utility models.

  5. Battle Engines: Large and powerful constructs, battle engines are war machines with unique abilities that can drastically impact the battlefield.

  6. Colossals: These are massive warjacks that tower over regular ones. Colossals are extremely powerful and serve as formidable assets in an army

  7. Structures: These models represent fortifications, buildings, or other constructs on the battlefield. They can provide cover and other advantages to their faction.
