Warmachine

Warmachine is a tabletop miniatures wargame published by Privateer Press, a gaming company known for its distinctive and immersive settings. The game is set in the Iron Kingdoms, a fantasy world that blends elements of steampunk, magic, and high adventure. In Warmachine, players take on the role of powerful warcasters, leaders gifted with magical abilities and the ability to control mighty warjacks, which are massive steam-powered mechanical constructs.
