Hordes Fractions

  • Trollbloods

    The Trollbloods are a hardy and resilient faction of trollkin warriors fighting to protect their ancient lands. They rely on the strength of their tough warbeasts, support from shamans, and powerful warlocks to outlast and overwhelm their enemies.

  • Circle Orboros

    The Circle Orboros is a secretive organization of druids and werewolves who seek to maintain the balance of nature. They wield the power of the elements, shapeshifting abilities, and fierce warbeasts to achieve their goals.

  • Skorne

    The Skorne Empire is an aggressive and militaristic faction, driven by their desire for conquest and glory. They control imposing warbeasts, powerful magic, and use pain as a resource to fuel their abilities.

  • Legion of Everblight

    The Legion of Everblight is a dragonspawn cult led by the dragon Everblight. They seek to spread Everblight's blight and corruption throughout the Iron Kingdoms. They use the power of dragonspawn, dragon-like warbeasts, and powerful sorcery.

    A secretive and enigmatic faction, the Retribution of Scyrah fights to protect their homeland and magical artifacts. Their forces rely on fast-moving warjacks, skilled mage hunters, and specialized infantry. They are masters of precision and surgical strikes.
