Hordes

Hordes is a tabletop miniatures wargame published by Privateer Press, set in the same rich and immersive fantasy world as Warmachine, known as the Iron Kingdoms. While Warmachine focuses on armies led by warcasters and their mechanized warjacks, Hordes centers around the primal power of warlocks and their savage warbeasts. Hordes provides an engaging and thrilling wargaming experience, focusing on the primal power of warlocks and their warbeasts in the savage and fantastical setting of the Iron Kingdoms. Whether you prefer commanding hulking beasts or unleashing the primal fury of nature, Hordes offers a unique and dynamic gaming experience within the realm of tabletop wargaming.
